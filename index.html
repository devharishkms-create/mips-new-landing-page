Plastic vs Wooden Pallets

Lighter Loads
Less Emissions

Compared to traditional wood block pallets which have a limited lifespan of 25 trips, MIPS plastic pallets complete an average of 100 trips (actual, not just lab tested) and have 28% less impact on global warming.

For every 100,000 MIPS pallets shipped, around 1,000 gallons of fuel are saved, reducing more than 20,000 pounds of greenhouse emissions.
