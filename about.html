About Us

MIPS provides the essential backbone for modern logistics and material handling. Our rental pallets, FLCs keep goods moving efficiently across industries — from manufacturing and retail to warehousing and distribution.

By offering smart rental solutions instead of one-time use products, MIPS helps businesses reduce waste, cut costs, and simplify operations. Our customers gain access to a reliable pool of high-quality assets without the burden of ownership or maintenance.

Backed by technology-driven tracking and sustainable practices, MIPS delivers operational, financial, and environmental advantages that traditional purchase-and-discard models can’t match. We make supply chains cleaner, safer, and more efficient — helping companies focus on what they do best, while we handle the movement that keeps everything connected.
