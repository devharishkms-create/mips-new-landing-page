Our Services

At MIPS Plastics, we provide smart, sustainable, and technology-driven logistics solutions to empower supply chains across India.

Pooling

At MIPS, our pooling services — including pallet and container pooling with FLC (Foldable Large Container) — handle all your packaging needs efficiently. We manage, clean, repair, and deliver reusable assets precisely when and where you need them.

  • Shared, reusable, food-safe packaging solutions
  • Reduces waste and boosts supply chain efficiency
  • On-time delivery and collection of pooled assets
  • Environmentally responsible with lower carbon footprint

Warehouse Management System (WMS)

MIPS provides an innovative Warehouse Management Software that simplifies operations for businesses of all sizes. Our WMS delivers real-time intelligence and integrates with any ERP for a seamless experience.

  • Automates entries, minimizes paperwork & errors
  • Supports barcode & RFID scanning
  • Automatic GRN & purchase order uploads
  • Multi-location & internal transfer management
  • Real-time dashboards and MIS reports

Asset Management

MIPS Pallet Pooling System offers a complete asset management solution designed to reduce operational costs and optimize logistics. Our system enables efficient tracking, utilization, and recovery of pallets across the supply chain — ensuring every asset adds maximum value.

  • Real-time pallet tracking with RFID & barcode technology
  • Minimized loss and better asset utilization
  • Automated repair and cleaning cycles
  • Integrated reporting for transparent insights
