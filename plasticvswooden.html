Plastic Pallets vs Wooden Pallets

Understand why MIPS plastic pallets outperform traditional wooden pallets.

Advantages of Plastic Pallets

  • Exceptional durability — lasts much longer than wooden pallets.
  • Lightweight yet strong — easy to handle and transport.
  • Hygienic and moisture-resistant — ideal for food and pharma industries.
  • Requires no repair or re-treatment — low maintenance costs.
  • 100% recyclable — supports sustainability and circular economy.

Disadvantages of Wooden Pallets

  • Prone to breakage and splintering under heavy or repeated loads.
  • Requires frequent maintenance, repairs, and replacements.
  • Absorbs moisture — can harbor bacteria, pests, and mold.
  • Heavier weight increases transport costs and handling time.
  • Non-uniform size and quality reduce automation compatibility.
